## Slide 3: Solution
**OCG Capital: Institutional Litigation Finance Powered by AI**

We bridge the capital gap by providing non-recourse funding to pre-screened litigation claims, generating uncorrelated returns for our investors.

**Our Approach:**
*   **Diversified Portfolio:** We deploy capital across a broad spectrum of pre-vetted claims, neutralizing the binary risk of any single case outcome.
*   **AI-Driven Case Selection:** We don't just rely on lawyer opinions. Our proprietary AI engine analyzes millions of historical dockets, judge rulings, and opposing counsel profiles to quantify win probabilities and expected timelines with statistical rigor.
*   **Non-Recourse Structure:** If a funded case loses, our capital is wiped out—but our upside on winning cases delivers the outsized returns required for top-quartile performance.
*   **Alignment of Interests:** We only profit when the plaintiff and attorney profit, ensuring rigorous front-end due diligence.

## Slide 5: Business Model
**Proven Alternative Fund Economics**

OCG Capital operates a classic closed-end fund structure optimized for illiquid, event-driven assets:

*   **Management Fee:** 2% annually on committed capital (covers legal due diligence, AI platform development, and operational overhead).
*   **Performance Carry:** 20% over an 8% preferred hurdle rate. We only win big when our investors beat their cost of capital first.
*   **Fund Life:** 5 Years (with two 1-year extensions at the GP's discretion to maximize recovery on late-stage cases).
*   **Revenue Mechanism:** We acquire an assigned interest in the claim's proceeds. Typical claim returns yield 3x-5x multiples on funded capital, blending down to a portfolio-level target.
*   **Capital Deployment:** Drawn down as cases hit specific litigation milestones (e.g., filing, discovery completion, trial prep), ensuring capital isn't sitting idle.
