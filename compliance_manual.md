## Slide 2: The Problem
**A Massive, Fragmented Market Starved for Institutional Capital**

*   **$17B+ Market, Highly Fragmented:** The litigation finance market is large but dominated by small, single-case boutique financiers lacking institutional scale.
*   **Misaligned Incentives:** Traditional funders often take concentrated bets on 1-2 cases, leading to binary outcomes and high volatility for investors.
*   **Institutional Demand is Growing:** Pension funds, endowments, and family offices are actively seeking uncorrelated returns to buffer against public market volatility, yet access to top-tier litigation assets remains severely limited.
*   **Information Asymmetry:** Law firms and corporates struggle to find reliable, well-capitalized partners who can deploy capital quickly and efficiently across multiple claims.

## Slide 5: Business Model
**Proven Alternative Asset Fund Economics**

*   **Management Fee: 2%** on committed capital during the investment period, stepping down to 1.5% on invested capital thereafter. Covers operational, legal review, and AI development costs.
*   **Carried Interest: 20%** above the hurdle rate. Aligns GP and LP interests for maximum upside capture.
*   **Hurdle Rate: 8% Preferred Return:** LPs receive an 8% compounding return on their capital before GP participation kicks in, ensuring strong downside protection and priority of returns.
*   **Capital Efficiency:** Litigation finance requires no physical assets; capital is deployed directly into legal claims with contractually defined payout multipliers (typically 2x-5x on funded amounts).

## Slide 6: Investment Strategy
**Disciplined Allocation for Non-Correlated Alpha**

**Portfolio Construction:**
*   Target Portfolio Size: 50-60 cases per fund vintage.
*   Max Single-Case Exposure: <5% of total fund capital.
*   Average Case Duration: 18-24 months (allowing for capital recycling).

**Case Type Allocation:**
*   **Commercial Disputes (50%):** Breach of contract, IP infringement, fraud. High dollar value, backed by strong documentary evidence.
*   **International Arbitration (25%):** Treaty claims and cross-border commercial disputes. High enforceability, sovereign/corporate respondents.
*   **Antitrust & Competition (15%):** Price-fixing and market manipulation claims. Statutory treble damages provide massive upside.
*   **Portfolio Financing (10%):** Funding a basket of cases for a single top-tier law firm. Provides immediate diversification and predictable deployment.
