Frequently Asked Questions

What is a fix and flip loan?

A fix and flip loan is a short-term financing option designed specifically for real estate investors to purchase, renovate, and quickly resell a property for a profit.

How fast can OCG Capital close a fix and flip loan?

We can typically close a fix and flip loan within 5 to 10 business days, depending on how quickly the required property documentation and appraisals are submitted.

What are the eligibility requirements for a fix and flip loan?

Eligibility is primarily based on the property's after-repair value (ARV) and the borrower's track record. We require a minimum credit score, a detailed scope of work, and a realistic renovation budget.

How are renovation funds disbursed during a fix and flip project?

Renovation funds are disbursed in draws. As the investor completes specific phases of the rehab project, an inspector verifies the work, and the corresponding draw is released to fund the next stage of renovations.
